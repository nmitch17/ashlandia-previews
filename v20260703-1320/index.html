Three Entrances into Ashlandia

Step through the one
that recognizes you.

Ashlandia is an invisible storyworld made of local lore, historical walks, creative rituals, seasonal adventures, and the small moments of wonder that appear when you begin paying attention. Each gate offers a different first adventure.

You may begin as a local, a visitor, or a creator, but once you are inside, the paths cross. Locals become guides. Visitors become storytellers. Creators become caretakers of the town beneath the town. Choose the entrance that feels most alive today.

Explore the Five Districts

Five districts.
Endless crossings.

Each district is real ground with an invisible character. The map shows you where you are. The stories show you where you could go. Start with a place, then follow the trail that catches your curiosity.

In season now: The Superhero Realm — July. Every realm can be found year-round. This is the one the whole town is playing right now.

The realms do not stay in their boxes. Each has a home district and a season of its own, but they unfurl across the whole town — there is a gnome in every home and business, if you look. And here is the secret: the more we explore these invisible realms, the more permanent they become. You are not visiting a finished world. You are helping it become real.

District 01

Downtown

The gathering place

Light the lantern to see where Ashlandia breaks through.

Real ground

The visible town gathers around the Plaza. World-class restaurants, creekside tables, charming shops, and cafes where the whole town seems to pass through eventually. The Plaza is the heart, and the creek runs right behind it.

Where Ashlandia breaks through

Stories gather here too — exchanged under clocks, tucked into alleyways, carried across the creek. This is where the Crow Lights village flickers on after dark, where a wizard school holds its lessons in plain sight, and where the downtown gnomes keep their headquarters — though by now there’s a gnome in every home and business in Ashland, if you know how to spot one.

Follow a trail Take the Food trail from cafe to creekside table, or the Spirit trail through the alleyways where the gnomes leave their marks. →

District 02

Railroad

The Maker Quarter

Light the lantern to see where Ashlandia breaks through.

Real ground

The district built where the rails met — where things were forged, shipped, and made, and where the makers still gather. The Golden Spike marks where the line was joined. The Historic Ashland Armory anchors the quarter, a hall built for gathering.

Where Ashlandia breaks through

The Armory throws open its doors for the Monster Ball. And somewhere in the streets around it, one of Ashlandia’s cryptid trails begins — for those willing to track what isn’t supposed to be here.

Follow a trail Pick up the cryptid trail at dusk, when the maker quarter is quietest and the tracks are easiest to read. →

District 03

Theater

The dramatic heart

Light the lantern to see where Ashlandia breaks through.

Real ground

The district that turns everything into a stage. The Oregon Shakespeare Festival and the Green Show stand at the center, with the Historic Ashland Springs Hotel keeping watch over it all. The town has gathered here to watch stories told out loud for nearly a century.

Where Ashlandia breaks through

The Fairytale Theater opens its curtain here, and the Time Traveler Hotel keeps rooms for guests arriving from the past and future. Here, the line between performance and reality has always been thin.

Follow a trail Take the Culture trail from the Green Show through the playhouses, and see which performances are only visible from inside Ashlandia. →

District 04

Green and Wild

The green threshold

Light the lantern to see where Ashlandia breaks through.

Real ground

Where the town softens into forest and the wild begins. Lithia Park and Ashland Creek form the green heart, with the Japanese Gardens tucked inside. The trail keeps rising from here — all the way to the summit of Mt. Ashland, 13.5 miles up through Ashland’s forests for those who answer the call.

Where Ashlandia breaks through Superhero Bootcamp is in season

This is the training grounds of the Superhero Bootcamp — but the realm is a physical one, and it doesn’t stay in the gardens. It lives in the trails, the fitness clubs, the spas, anywhere the body is tended and tested. The Fairy Godmother Tea Party gathers here too. Cross the threshold and the wild starts telling you things.

Follow a trail Start with the Nature trail along the creek, or commit to the long ascent — a full day’s quest to the mountaintop. →

District 05

University

The realm of ideas

Light the lantern to see where Ashlandia breaks through.

Real ground

Where the town goes to learn, question, and imagine. Southern Oregon University anchors the district, with ScienceWorks, the Ashland Y, and gathering spaces for every kind of learner.

Where Ashlandia breaks through Superhero Bootcamp is in season

The Mystery Museum opens its cases here. The Sea of Creativity and the Treasure Bazaar appear for those seeking them, and Superhero Bootcamp trains a cohort on this side of town, too — the physical realm reaches everywhere people push themselves. Ideas have a way of becoming real in this district.

Follow a trail Take the Mindfulness trail through the museum, or follow your curiosity to the Treasure Bazaar and see what treasure you can trade for. →

The districts hold the ground. The realms move across it.

Walk one district with the lantern lit and you will start to see it everywhere: the town beneath the town, waiting to be played.
