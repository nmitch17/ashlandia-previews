Artnow

Re-enchanting everyday life through community art

Artnow is a 501(c)(3) cultural nonprofit based in Ashland, Oregon. We build immersive, place-based story experiences that turn neighbors into collaborators and public spaces into landscapes of wonder.

We believe the stories we tell together shape our world. Right now, too many of those stories divide us, sell to us, or ask us only to watch. Artnow makes different ones — larger-than-life, participatory, and rooted in this place — that invite people of every age and background to step in and create.

The Secret Storyworld

Our creative engine. A storygame platform that anchors stories in real time and real place: a calendar of twelve monthly realms that gives families and communities the tools to read, write, and play their way into new possibilities — building creativity, connection, and agency along the way.

☾

Ashlandia

The Secret Storyworld brought to life across an entire town. A year-round participatory theater of place, Ashlandia recasts Ashland as an interactive landscape of twelve realms, each with its own mythology, quests, and seasonal gatherings. Streets, parks, and storefronts become a story the whole community writes together.

Discovery Trails

Self-guided story walks through Ashland’s real history and hidden wonders, built with the City of Ashland and local historians. The first trail, Where the Crow Lights, opens September 2026.

✎

Wonderland Creator Lab

After-school game design and world-building for middle schoolers, where young people learn to build the worlds they want to live in.

❖

Seasonal festivals & gatherings

Wizard Walk, the Faery Godmother Tea Party, Creature Crawl, Gnomecoming, and the community events that mark Ashland’s year.

The Story So Far

Twelve storygames and counting.

Twelve storygames produced, alongside the community events and ceremonies that mark Ashland’s year. We work in partnership with the City of Ashland, Southern Oregon University, and local schools and businesses.

Artnow is in residency at the White Rabbit Creator Clubhouse — our home for gallery exhibitions, programming, and community gatherings.

Meet the Crew

Producers, builders,
and storytellers.

Artnow is steered by a small crew who bring decades of regional arts, publishing, and technology experience to the work.

Captain (Executive Director)

Producer of regional PBS documentaries (Crater Lake: Mirror of Heaven, South Road to Oregon, Travel Oregon Scenic Byways, Asante Heartbeats) and community events including Ashland’s Festival of Light, the Children’s Halloween Parade, ScienceWorks’ After Dark series, and the annual lantern processions in Lithia Park.

Jane Almquist

Mapmaker (Creative Producer)

Owner of TreeHouse Books and creator of numerous businesses and product lines, including Modern Faery, Wizard Apothecary, Tiny Treasures, and Ashlandia. Co-producer, with Cynthia, of twelve storygames and countless community events.
