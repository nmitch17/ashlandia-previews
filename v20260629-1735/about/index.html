The Lore

Before our world, the Secret World

…in the past and in the future there were powerful Magical Creatures who lived in a Secret Invisible World — a world that existed long before ours. Some believe it was the Magical Creatures who made our world, using the energies of the Secret World, moving between the two through enchanted gateways and portals.

As time passed, the creatures Owl and Raven — guardians of the Moon and the Sun — wrote down the secrets of the invisible energy that creates worlds. A Book of Secrets: how to master time, cast magic word spells, shapeshift, tame magical animals, manifest treasures. That book is the one you write in.

In the visible world

Who builds Ashlandia

Ashlandia is a place-based community storygame in Ashland, Oregon — a project of ArtNow, founded by artisans of the Oregon Shakespeare Festival, and the Ashlandia.org nonprofit. Headquartered at the White Rabbit Creator Clubhouse, we blend immersive storytelling, seasonal festivals, and daily creative practice to help people tell new stories — for their lives, their town, and the world.
