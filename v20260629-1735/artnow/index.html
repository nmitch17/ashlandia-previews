ArtNow

Re-enchanting our lives and world through community art NOW!

Artnow is a 501(c)(3) arts nonprofit based in Ashland, Oregon. We build immersive, place-based story experiences that turn neighbors into collaborators and public spaces into landscapes of wonder.

We believe the stories we tell together shape our world. Right now, too many of those stories divide us, sell to us, or ask us only to watch. Artnow makes different ones — larger-than-life, participatory, and rooted in this place — that invite people of every age and background to step in and create.

The Secret Storyworld

Artnow’s creative engine is The Secret Storyworld — a storygame platform that anchors stories in real time and real place. Using a calendar of monthly themes, it gives families and communities the tools to read, write, and play their way into new possibilities, building creativity, connection, and agency along the way.

☾

Ashlandia

Ashlandia is The Secret Storyworld brought to life across an entire town.

A year-round participatory theatre of place, Ashlandia recasts Ashland as an interactive landscape of twelve realms — one for every month of the year, each with its own mythology, quests, and challenges. Through immersive storytelling, site-specific art, and seasonal events, it transforms streets, parks, and storefronts into a story the whole community writes together.
