Your Passport

How the journey works

I

The invitation arrives

Each month, an envelope. It names the realm, shares its lore, and invites you to accept or refuse the call. Everyone is invited to the same game.

II

Thirty days in the realm

Write your story a page a day in the Book of Secrets. Daily prompts help when you want them and vanish when you don't.

III

The stamp

Complete the month and the realm's stamp joins your passport — twelve stamps, one full turn of the wheel, one hero's journey.

The year follows the hero's journey down the chakra path — descending from idea (crystal, the crown, January) into matter and home (red, the root, December). Each realm carries a genre and a life practice: goal setting, kindness, inspiration, mindfulness, relationships, fitness, rest, creativity, gratitude, celebration. You aren't just writing a story. You're rewriting one.
