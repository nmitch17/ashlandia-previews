Your first micro-quest

The One-Block Quest

Here is the first creator’s exercise of Ashlandia:

Pick one block in Ashland. Design a quest that fits on it.

A question taped inside a window. A character who only appears at dusk. A treasure that is just a beautiful sentence. Keep it small enough to happen and strange enough to matter.

The door needs at least a sentence to draw itself. Start with the block.

A new door exists because you imagined it.

Every quest in Ashlandia started exactly this way — one person, one block, one idea. Bring it to the White Rabbit Creator Clubhouse and we will help you build it for real.
